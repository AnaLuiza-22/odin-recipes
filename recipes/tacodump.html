Chicken Taco Dump

This is a Taco Dump Dinner recipe

This features chicken, beans and salsa all layered with corn tortilla crisps and cheese. And to finish it off, you may add any taco topings as you may like such as sour cream, guacamole, lettuce, cilantro and many more.

  • Chunky Salsa
  • Green chilies
  • Chili powder
  • Ground cumin
  • Salt
  • Chicken
  • Black beans
  • Corn
  • Corn crisps
  • Cheese(of your choice)
