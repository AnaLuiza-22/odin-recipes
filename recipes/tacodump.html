Chicken Taco Dump

This is a Taco Dump Dinner recipe

This features chicken, beans and salsa all layered with corn tortilla crisps and cheese. And to finish it off, you may add any taco topings as you may like such as sour cream, guacamole, lettuce, cilantro and many more.

  • 16 ounces of chunky salsa
  • 4 ounces of green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 5 cups of shredded cooked chicken
  • 15 ounces of black beans
  • 1 1/2 cups of fire-roasted corn
  • A bag of corn crisps
  • 3 cups of shredded cheese(of your choice)
  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 190 degrees C and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray or butter.
  3. Combine the salsa, green chilies, chili powder, cumin, and salt in a large bowl. Stir in the chicken, black beans, and corn.
  4. Put the corn tortilla crisps at the bottom until it covers the surface. Add half the chicken mixture. Repeat adding layers of the tortilla crips and the chicken mixture until finished.
  5. Cover dish with foil and put it in the oven to bake for 35 minutes.
  6. Remove the foil and add the remaining cheese. Continue to bake until cheese is melted and starting to brown, around 10 minutes.
  7. Serve with desired taco toppings!!!
